How to Style Our Favorites Together

The magic of our product collection is how beautifully each piece works with the others. A soy candle sitting beside a ceramic vase filled with dried lavender, placed on a woven tray — that's a vignette that stops you in your tracks every time you walk past it.

The Perfect Shelf Vignette

Try grouping our ceramic vase, a pillar candle, and a small dried floral bundle together on a bookshelf or side table. Vary the heights, let the textures play off each other, and add a small trailing plant or crystal for a finishing touch. Simple, beautiful, uniquely yours.

The Cozy Corner Setup

Place one of our larger woven baskets beside an armchair to hold throw blankets. Add a candle on the side table beside a stack of your favorite books. Tuck a small dried floral arrangement nearby. You've just created the coziest reading corner imaginable.
