About BloomNestHome

The people, the passion, and the philosophy behind the shop

🏡

We Believe Home Should Feel Like You

BloomNestHome started with a simple idea: that a home should feel warm, personal, and beautifully intentional. Not a showroom. Not a catalog page. A real, lived-in space that reflects the personality of the person living there.

We opened our Etsy shop to share the products we personally loved — handpicked pieces that add warmth and soul to any space: soy candles, botanical prints, ceramic-style vases that look handcrafted without the price.

The blog came next, naturally. Because we found ourselves talking so much about home decor — the little tricks that make a big difference, the styling principles that designers use, the seasonal updates that keep a home feeling fresh.

What You'll Find Here

This blog is a free resource for anyone who loves their home and wants to make it even more beautiful — without needing a big budget or a design degree. We write about decor tips, bookshelf styling, seasonal refreshes, and product recommendations we genuinely stand behind.

The Shop

Our Etsy shop, BloomNestHome, is the heart of everything we do. Every product is carefully chosen to bring warmth and beauty into your home.
